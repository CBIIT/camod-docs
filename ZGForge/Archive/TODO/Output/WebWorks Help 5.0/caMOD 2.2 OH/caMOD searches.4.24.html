Pre-Clinical/Clinical Trials Drug Screening Results
To launch a search for pre-clinical or clinical trials drug screening results, see Drug Screening Search. Once the search is initiated, the results of pre-clinical and/or clinical trials performed with the specified drug display on a Search Results page in a summary table, with details listed for each type of search under the summary table.
