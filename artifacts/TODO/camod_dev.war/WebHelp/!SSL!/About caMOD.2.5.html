Logging into caMOD
When you click the URL http://cancermodels.nci.nih.gov to access caMOD and indicate your agreement on the LEGAL RULES OF THE ROAD page, the login page displays.
1.
Enter the login information, the ID and Password. Click the Login button.
2.
If you do not have a Login ID nor Password, click the Register button to apply for a user account. For more information, see Creating a New User Account.
See also User Settings.
