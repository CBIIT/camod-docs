Using Online Help
The online help has been written to help you master the caMOD software.
To access online help, use any of the following methods:
•
Many fields in caMOD forms display a Help icon (). Pause your cursor over the icon to open a tool tip.
•
Click with your mouse on the Help icon () to open a context sensitive topic.
•
Click the Help hypertext link in the left sidebar to open the complete online help project.
•
Click the Help menu option in the right of the gray bar just below the caMOD logo on the viewer screen.
•
Click the Help button that displays on the caMOD login form .
Once you are in online help, an array of options helps you locate topics of interest .
•
Context sensitive help displays online help topics that correspond to the “form” or page from which help was opened. Click the Show hypertext link at the top of the topics to open the full online help directory.
•
Follow hypertext links, Related Topics or See Also buttons in the help topics to open other closely related topics.
•
Locate topics using the table of contents that displays in the left pane of the online help project or the Index tab that displays at the top of your screen.
•
Perform word searches by entering query text in the search text box.
