Submitting and Editing Models > Transplant/Xenograft
Transplant/Xenograft
To enter transplant/xenograft information to a submitted model,
1.
Click Xenograft from the list of Submit and Edit Models menu options on the left side of the caMOD viewing window. These menu options only display after you have clicked the (model) Submit button in other caMOD viewers.
2.
Click the Enter Transplant/Xenograft heading.
3.
In the Xenograft/Transplantation dialog box, enter or select the appropriate information about your animal model in the text entry fields. Items with an aster­isk are required fields.
 
Xenograft/Transplantation Fields
Description
*Cell Line/Transplant
Enter the name of the Transplant or Cell Line. In some cases, this is the name of the cell line that is being inserted.
*Donor Species
Select the Donor Species from the drop-down list.
If Other Species
Enter Donor Species that is not listed in the drop-down list.
Note: This field is only writable after you select Other in the previous field.
Donor Strain
A specific breeding population of mice, including inbred strains, progeny of specific genetic crosses, or a mixed population of mice with partially known or unknown genetic background. (Source: Jackson Laboratory MGI Glossary)
 
If applicable, select the Donor Species from the drop-down list. If the appropriate species is not listed, select Other and enter the Other Donor Species in the text field under the list.
Other Strain
Enter Donor Strain that is not listed in the drop-down list. Note: This field is only writable after you select Other in the previous field.
Organ/Tissue
Click the Select button. Select from the Organ/Tissue vocabulary tree that opens the organ or tissue in which the transgene, the genomic segment or the targeted modification was found.
*Graft Type
Select from the drop-down list. If the appropriate Graft Type is not listed, select Other from the drop-down list and enter the Other Graft Type in the following text box.
If Other Graft Type
Enter Graft Type that is not listed in the drop-down list. Note: This field is only writable after you select Other in the previous field.
Parental Cell Line
Enter the name or designator of the Parental Cell Line to be used in the experiment.
ATTC Number (if available)
Enter the unique identifier of the cell line in the American Type Culture Collection (ATCC). If you don't know the number, click the Find ATCC # button. Once you have found the number, copy the number and paste it onto the field on the Transplant/Xenograft page.
Genetic Alteration
Describe the genetic manipulations used in the transplant or xenograft.
Method of Modification
Describe the modification method(s) used for the cell line or transplant. Note: This field becomes writable only after you have entered information in the Genetic Alteration field.
Amount of Cells
Enter the number or amount of cells transplanted into the animal.
Growth Period
Enter the period of time the graft was allowed to grow.
Site of Administration
Select from the drop-down list the body part in which the graft was placed (intraperitoneal, subcutaneous). If the appropriate site is not listed, select Other, and enter the Other Site of Administration in the text field under the list.
Other Site of Administration
Enter Site of Administration that is not listed in the drop-down list. Note: This field is only writable after you select Other in the previous field.
Host Species/Strain
The non-editable text in this field are copies of the species and strain information you entered on the Model Characteristics page.
4.
Click the Submit button to add the information to your model.
5.
Click Reset to return the form to default settings: If you are entering data for the first time, Reset clears the form (resets it to its defaults). If you are changing data for an existing model, Reset returns the form back to the unmodified state (the default).
