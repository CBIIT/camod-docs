Deployment steps

a. caimage.ftp.modelstoragedirectory=Images/images/Model_Images/Model/
b. caimage.ftp.genconstoragedirectory=Images/images/Model_Images/GeneticConstruct/
c. caimage.modelview.uri=http://caimage.nci.nih.gov/lizardtech/Model_Images/Model/
d. caimage.genconview.uri=http://caimage.nci.nih.gov/lizardtech/Model_Images/GeneticConstruct/
